    What is the difference between RGB, RGBW and RGBWW?

    RGB: it has, red, green and blue LEDs, produces red, green and blue light and the combinations of these 3 colors. White light is produced by the 3 LEDs together, so it will look more or less blue.

    RGBW: it has all functionality of RGB, plus it has extra white LEDs which can give out cool white light color. White light is produced by the pure white LEDs only, so it will not look blue, but cool white.

    RGBWW: it has all functionality of RGB, plus it has extra warm white LEDs which can give out warm white light color. White light is produced by the pure warm white LEDs only, so it will not look blue, but warm white.
