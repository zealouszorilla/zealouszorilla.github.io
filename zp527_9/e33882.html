FAQ:
Is it necessary to print TPU at a low speed?
As TPU is soft and flexible, the nozzle may shift away the first layers if printed at a high speed. A consistent and slow printing speed is key to successful printing with TPU.

Is it necessary to enable retraction?
When your design requires the extruder to move a long gap without printing, sometimes it needs to be retracted. However, when using flexible 3D printing materials, retraction can be particularly troublesome because they can be sensitive to rapid movements. In this case, we recommend you to disable retraction for the first time. Otherwise, you can minimize the retraction setting if you need.
