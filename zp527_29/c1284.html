China and the Writing of English Literary Modernity, 1690-1770

著者名: Eun Kyung Min (Seoul National University) 出版者: Cambridge University Press 出版年: 19 Apr 2018 版表示: シリーズ: PaperbackThis book shows that the self-conscious construction of ideas about modern English literary character derived in part from debates about Chinese history, taste, and culture. By writing China into new literary forms such as the novel, periodical paper, and newspaper, writers helped define what constituted modern English identity.
