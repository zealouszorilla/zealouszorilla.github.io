The Romantic Tavern: Literature and Conviviality in the Age of Revolution (Cambridge Studies in Romanticism: Series Number 125)

著者名: Ian Newman (University of Notre Dame Indiana) 出版者: Cambridge University Press 出版年: 28 Mar 2019 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackThis study of Romantic era London taverns explores metropolitan political and cultural life, focusing on architecture and convivial practice, including drinking songs, toasting practices, Anacreontic poetry and political ballads. It will appeal to literary scholars, historians, musicologists, and anyone interested in the history of the British pub.
