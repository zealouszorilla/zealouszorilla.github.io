The Black Crowes - "The Southern Harmony And Musical Companion" / Super Deluxe 3 CD boxset includes a hymn book with insights from both Chris and Rich Robinson, sheet music and 4 lithographs. The reissue was overseen by Chris and Rich Robinson and album producer George Drakoulias. Featuring 14 unreleased tracks - including 2 studio recordings, a live February 1993 concert, and the never-before- heard live in-studio performances at the end of the Southern Harmony session - along with B-sides and the original album.
