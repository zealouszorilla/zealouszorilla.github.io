コメント：The legendary Pink Floyd concert film P.U.L.S.E. is now being re-released with video footage newly re-edited from the original master tapes specifically for the 2019 The Later Years box set. For this release, the cover design has received an update with photos from Aubrey Powell / Hipgnosis and Rupert Truman / StormStudios, and the packaging reintroduces the iconic pulsating light from the original CD release in 1995 - this time with 2 replaceable AA batteries. The deluxe package includes music videos, concert films, documentaries, recordings of the Pulse Tour rehearsals and much more. In addition, a large booklet is included. All Pink Floyd live releases are highly regarded, but few are as brilliant and well-produced as P.U.L.S.E., which stands as the last great live document for Pink Floyd"s entire career. The concert is also remarkable for a complete live performance of the masterpiece Dark Side Of The Moon in it"s full length.
