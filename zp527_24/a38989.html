TST Industries exclusive undertail closeout for the 2019+ Kawasaki ZX6R closes the hole left behind after removing the OEM fender. Crafted from CNC machined aluminum and given a durable black anodized finish，this undertail closeout will gives your ZX6R a sleek and race-inspired look while preventing debris and road grime from entering your subtray.

When you get rid of that bulky OEM fender，you’re left with a hole that can easily let water，road debris，and more into the subtray of your 2019+ Kawasaki ZX6R. This is why we created an easy to install undertail closeout that covers the hole and gives your undertail a sleek and race inspired look. We custom designed the closeout to perfectly fit the undertail fairing and CNC machined the design from high-grade aluminum. The closeout has a durable black anodized finish and comes with corrosion resistant hardware to make installation a breeze. This lightweight undertail closeout kit is perfect for track enthusiasts as well as street riders that have moved their license plate bracket to a non-OEM location.
