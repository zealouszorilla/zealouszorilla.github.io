Def Leppard’s 40th anniversary reissue of Pyromania has been expanded as a 4-CD set with unheard demos and live shows from the era. Also included is a Blu-ray containing a Dolby Atmos mix of the album, 5 music videos, and a book with new interviews and rare photos by Ross Halfin. In tribute to this milestone, this deluxe edition has been executive produced by lead singer Joe Elliott and Ronan McHugh.(デフレパード)
