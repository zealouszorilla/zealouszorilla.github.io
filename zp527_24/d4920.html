How to pair
In the off status, press and hold MFB for 5-7 seconds to enter pairing mode, the LED flashes blue and red alternately. Search and click to connect KH-23 on the Bluetooth list of your device, the LED flashes blue when connected.

Connection with Two devices
After the first connection is successful, turn off the Bluetooth on the first device, KH-23 will enter pairing mode automatically. Turn on the Bluetooth on the second device, search "KH-23" and click to connect. Turn on the Bluetooth on the first device, click "KH-23" and connect the Bluetooth headset.
