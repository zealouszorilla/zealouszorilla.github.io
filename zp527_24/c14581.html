【本の内容】
Peyrassol located in the Massif des Maures France has a settlement history dating back a long way and it was here where the Knights Templar were to establish one of their commanderies. This huge 1000ha domain has now been restored to its past splendor. Investing a great amount of passion its present owner Philippe Austruy has turned Peyrassol into what it used to be - a place of great beauty yet with a commercial purpose. Long stone terraces once again underline the symmetry of the vineyards offsetting the ancient oak trees - a setting evoking the spirit of the Knights Templar. And art du vin is not the sole attraction in Peyrassol: Philippe Austruy has also created one of France"s most beautiful contemporary sculpture parks. Peyrassol a place where time stands still a place where past and present meet in an eternal symbiosis.
Text in English and French.
