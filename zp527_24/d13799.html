コメント:Super Deluxe Edition contains four CDs, Blu-Ray, and vinyl LP pressing. Includes Steven Wilson"s newly remastered version of the original album on both CD and LP. Two previously unreleased concerts from 1971 debut in the set as well. The first was recorded just a few weeks before The Yes Album was released, while the second was recorded six months later during the band"s U.S. tour. Wilson features the 2014 remixed The Yes Album and instrumental versions of all six album tracks. In addition, the Blu-ray disc offers four new mixes of The Yes Album, including two versions in 5.1 Surround Sound, the 2023 Remaster, and the Dolby Atmos Mix.
