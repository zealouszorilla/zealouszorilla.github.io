説明: Hyperikon introduces the 5” Random Orbit Sander to help you speed up your projects! The compact construction combines the functionality of a belt sander and orbital finishing sander into one powerful tool with a lightweight, portable design. The ON/OFF switch is sealed and dust protected for long-lasting performance. It is located at the front of the grip so that the sander can be easily activated and stopped by both right- and left-handed users.

With a hook and loop sanding pad, replacing sandpaper is done in no time. To adjust the sander for a hard or extra soft finishing operation, choose a sanding paper with appropriate grit and set the speed nob at the desired level.

APPLICATION

Use Hyperikon’s Random Orbit Sander for your next renovating, refurbishing or remodeling project. This power tool will prove itself very helpful for polishing coffee tables, counter tops, tables, benches, chairs, desks, stairs, or for removing wax, dirt build-up, stain, varnish, and old paint. The machine is intended for dry sanding of wood, plastic, l, filler as well as coated surfaces. Make sure to keep it away from wet working surfaces!
